FCC & TCPA Compliance Protocol

Lantern Lead Gen

Effective Date: July 20, 2025

1. Purpose

This protocol outlines how Lantern Lead Gen ensures compliance with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), Federal Communications Commission (FCC) regulations, and applicable state-level telemarketing laws. It is designed to protect consumer rights, promote ethical lead generation, and ensure that all lead buyers operate within legal boundaries.

2. Consent Collection

  • Affirmative Consent: All lead intake forms include an unchecked checkbox that users must select to confirm they have read and agree to the Privacy Policy.
  • Consent Language: The Privacy Policy clearly states that by submitting their information, users consent to be contacted by one or more certified life coaches affiliated with the specific TikTok creator or campaign they interacted with.
  • Consent Recordkeeping: For each submission, Lantern Lead Gen stores:
    • Timestamp of submission
    • Checkbox status
    • Source URL or campaign ID
    • Version of the Privacy Policy
  • Confirmation Messages: If a confirmation message is sent, it will be limited to one message within 5 minutes of submission and will contain no promotional content.

3. Do Not Contact (DNC) Compliance

  • Internal DNC List: Lantern Lead Gen maintains an internal Do Not Contact list. Individuals who request not to be contacted will be added and excluded from future lead sales.
  • National DNC Registry: All lead buyers must scrub their call lists against the National Do Not Call Registry before outreach.
  • State DNC Laws: Lead buyers must comply with all applicable state-level DNC laws, including those in states with enhanced consumer protections.

4. Permissible Contact Hours

  • Lead buyers may only contact leads between 8:00 AM and 9:00 PM local time of the recipient.

5. Consent Revocation

  • Users may revoke consent at any time via email, SMS, voicemail, or any reasonable method.
  • Revocation requests must be honored within 10 business days.
  • Lantern Lead Gen will test revocation handling through dummy leads and follow-up monitoring.

6. Lead Buyer Requirements

All lead buyers must:

  • Acknowledge and agree to this compliance protocol before receiving leads
  • Certify that they will:
    • Scrub leads against the National DNC Registry
    • Contact leads only during permitted hours
    • Honor revocation and opt-out requests promptly
    • Comply with all applicable state-level telemarketing laws
  • Agree to periodic audits, including the use of test leads

7. Data Privacy and Security

  • Data is stored in secure, access-controlled environments (e.g., encrypted Google Sheets)
  • Access is limited to authorized personnel
  • Data is shared only with certified life coaches who have agreed to this compliance protocol

8. Monitoring and Enforcement

  • Lantern Lead Gen actively monitors lead buyer behavior through:
    • Submission of test leads
    • Tracking of call timing and content
    • Testing of consent revocation handling
  • Violations may result in:
    • Suspension or termination of lead access
    • Reporting to regulatory authorities
    • Legal action by affected consumers
