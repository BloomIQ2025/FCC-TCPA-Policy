FCC & TCPA Compliance Protocol

Lantern Lead Gen

Effective Date: July 20, 2025

1. Purpose

This protocol outlines the procedures Lantern Lead Gen follows to ensure compliance with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), Federal Communications Commission (FCC) regulations, and applicable state-level telemarketing laws. It is designed to protect consumer rights, promote ethical lead generation, and ensure that all lead buyers operate within legal boundaries.

2. Consent Collection

  • Affirmative Consent: All lead intake forms include an unchecked checkbox that users must select to confirm they have read and agree to the Privacy Policy, which outlines how their information will be used.
  • Consent Language: The Privacy Policy clearly states that by submitting their information, users consent to be contacted by certified life coaches via phone, email, or text message.
  • Data Stored: For each submission, Lantern Lead Gen stores the timestamp of the form submission. No IP addresses or additional metadata are collected.

3. Do Not Call (DNC) Compliance

  • Internal DNC List: Lantern Lead Gen maintains an internal Do Not Contact (DNC) list. Any individual who requests not to be contacted will be added to this list and excluded from future lead sales.
  • National DNC Registry: All lead buyers are required to scrub their call lists against the National Do Not Call Registry prior to outreach.
  • State DNC Laws: Buyers must comply with all applicable state-level DNC laws, including those in states with enhanced consumer protections.

4. Permissible Calling Hours

  • Lead buyers may only contact leads between 8:00 AM and 9:00 PM local time of the recipient.
  • Lantern Lead Gen will periodically submit test (“dummy”) leads to monitor compliance with this requirement.

5. Consent Revocation

  • Users may revoke consent at any time by emailing lantern.leads@yahoo.com.
  • Lead buyers are required to honor revocation requests within 10 business days of notification.
  • Lantern Lead Gen will test revocation handling through dummy leads and follow-up monitoring.

6. Lead Buyer Requirements

All lead buyers must:

  • Acknowledge and agree to this compliance protocol prior to receiving leads.
  • Certify that they will:
    • Scrub leads against the National DNC Registry
    • Contact leads only during permitted hours
    • Honor revocation and opt-out requests promptly
  • Agree to periodic audits, including the use of test leads submitted by Lantern Lead Gen.

7. Data Privacy and Security

  • Lantern Lead Gen’s Privacy Policy outlines how user data is collected, used, and shared.
  • Data is stored in secure, access-controlled environments (e.g., Google Sheets) and is only accessible to authorized personnel.
  • Data is shared only with certified life coaches who have agreed to this compliance protocol.

8. Monitoring and Enforcement

  • Lantern Lead Gen actively monitors lead buyer behavior through:
    • Submission of test leads
    • Tracking of call timing and content
    • Testing of consent revocation handling
  • Violations of this protocol may result in:
    • Suspension or termination of lead access
    • Reporting to regulatory authorities
    • Legal action by affected consumers
